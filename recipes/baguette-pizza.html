Baguette-Pizza

Description

Take a regular baguette and split lengthwise to create a tasty pizza. Toppings can be as simple as sauce and cheese, or upgrade with pepperoni and sausage !

Ingredients

  • Baguette: It is absolutely necessary to use a fresh French baguette.
  • Sauce: You can use the sauce you want, we recommend a tomato pizza sauce.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella cheese make this lasagna extra decadent..
  • Toppings: Whatever you like.

Instructions

  1. Split the baguette lengthwise.
  2. Warm the sauce.
  3. Spread the sauce on the baguette.
  4. Add the cheese and toppings.
  5. Place the pizza on a ray and cook it in the oven : 375Â°F about 18 minutes.
